Surnames in Argentina

Last names are cultural traits that in most societies are spread patri and / or matrilineally through a vertical mechanism comparable to genetic transmission. This leads them to constitute a universal biological and sociocultural variable.

In this application you will find:

  • Different indicators calculated from the surnames:
    • By province
    • By department
      • Department detail
  • The distribution of a surname in the Argentine territory:
    • By province
    • By department
  • The distribution by origin geo-linguistic of surnames in the Argentine territory:
    • By province
    • By department

In addition, you can find information about the source of the surnames, the characteristics of the indicators and other questions to consider in the tab. "About"
